About

Brenon.Cloud is your launchpad for building a powerful, private, and reliable personal cloud. What started with a vision of affordable technological freedom on just two mini computers is now evolving. We're expanding to include robust services like identity management with Authentik and API orchestration with Kong, empowering you to create a truly integrated and secure digital ecosystem. Join us in shaping the future of decentralized, fast, and dependable personal cloud solutions.
